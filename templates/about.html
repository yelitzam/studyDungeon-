StudyDungeon



Having an academic support nextwork is crucial to college success, but it can be difficult to form one as a minority in your field. Today, women make up only 14% of the engineering workforce and less than 20% of engineering students. Combined with the fast-paced and competitive quarter system environment, this can leave many students without friends in their classes.

StudyDungeon is here to help UCSD students find people in their classes and form study groups - to get started, visit the "Search for a Class" page and enter a class you're enrolled in.

Our goal is to help everyone strengthen their academic network here at UCSD, and especially to be a resource for women and other minorities in STEM majors.
